# IBM-HR-cleaned-excel-analysis-and-tableau
Project Summary

This project analyzes employee workforce data to identify drivers of attrition, employee performance trends, and workforce risks using Excel for data analysis and Tableau for interactive visualization.
The objective was to transform raw HR data into actionable business insights that support better retention strategies and HR decision-making.

**Business Questions Answered**
What factors contribute most to employee attrition?
Which employee groups are at higher risk of leaving?
How do income, overtime, and tenure affect retention?
Does training improve employee outcomes?
Where are promotion bottlenecks occurring?

Overtime vs Attrition

Training & Promotion Insights
Employee Satisfaction Trends

**Key Insights**
Employees working overtime show higher attrition risk.
Promotion delays correlate with lower satisfaction.
Income distribution impacts retention patterns.
Certain departments require targeted retention strategies.
Training participation influences workforce stability.
